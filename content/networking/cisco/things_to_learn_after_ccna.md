What are supplementary skills that go well with CCNA for landing a job in networking? I went through dozens of job listings that mentioned CCNA. I Then, I tallied up the main devices/vendors, certifications, and technologies mentioned. I left out anything that wasn't mentioned more than twice.

I left out core CCNA technologies such as LAN, WAN, OSPF, Spanning Tree, VLANs, etc. The point here is to target the most sought after technologies and skills by employers. I also left out soft skills and any job that wasn't a networking specific role.
