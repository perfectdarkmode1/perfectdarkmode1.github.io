What are supplementary skills that go well with CCNA for landing a job in networking?

I went through Dozens of job listings that mentioned CCNA. I then tallied up devices/ vendors, certifications, and technologies mentioned. I left out anything that wasn’t mentioned more than twice.

I also left out core CCNA technologies such as LAN, WAN, OSPF, Spanning Tree, and VLANs. Also, The point here is to target the most sought after technologies and skills by employers.

And lastly, I left out soft skills and any job that wasn’t a networking specific role.
